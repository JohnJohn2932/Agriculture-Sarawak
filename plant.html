Plant Care Tips

Sunlight

Most plants in Sarawak need 6-8 hours of sunlight daily. Consider using shade nets during peak hours.

Watering

Water plants early morning or late evening. Adjust frequency based on rainfall and season.

Soil

Use well-draining soil rich in organic matter. Regular soil testing helps maintain optimal conditions.

Pest Control

Regular inspection and natural pest control methods are recommended for sustainable farming.
